Banana, Peanut Butter and Dates Smoothie

If there was ever a time that I could truly say how much I love a smoothie, this banana peanut butter and date smoothie would be it.

I make this smoothie daily. It's the perfect post-workout drink (you can easily add in vanilla protein powder to this)

or the perfect after-dinner treat to curb that sweet tooth.

Ingredients

  • 2x Bananas
  • 500ml Coconut Milk
  • 1X Tbsp Peanut Butter
  • 2x Dates
  • Chia seeds
  • Honey

Steps

  1. Blend all ingredients in a powerful blender and enjoy your drink!
