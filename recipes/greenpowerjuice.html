Green Power Juice

So many of those green juices are great for you, but they taste like a liquid salad.

If that's your sort of thing, good for you! The rest of us are jealous. Me? I need some sweetness, and a juice I can actually look forward to.

This celery, ginger, apple juice definitely checks all of the boxes.

Ingredients

  • 1x Celery
  • 1x Apple
  • 1x Cucumber
  • 1x Lime
  • Ginger

Steps

  1. Extract celery
  2. Extract cucumber
  3. Extract apple
  4. Extract ginger
  5. Extract lime
